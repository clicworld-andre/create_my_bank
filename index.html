Your Path to Community Banking

Here's exactly how to go from zero to running your own Community Social Bank

1

Download & Register

Start by downloading the Clix Wallet from the Play Store. Registration takes just minutes‚Äîverify your phone number, create a secure PIN, and you're ready to go. Your wallet is your gateway to community banking.

Quick setup Phone verification Secure PIN
2

Create Your Savings Group

From within the Clix Wallet, create your first savings group. Give it a name that reflects your community, set your savings goals, and establish the basic rules that will govern how your group operates.

Custom group name Set savings targets Define group rules
3

Invite Your Community

Share your group invite link with friends, family, neighbors, or colleagues. You need a minimum of 20 members to qualify for Community Social Bank status. Each member downloads the wallet and joins your group.

Share invite links 20 member minimum Track invitations
4

Practice Banking Together

This is where the magic happens. Your group can now save together, with members contributing regularly. Need funds? Apply for a group loan. This trial period lets everyone experience running a mini-bank before going full scale.

Group savings Micro-loans Build experience
5

Launch Your Community Social Bank

Once you have 20+ active members and have demonstrated consistent group activity, you're eligible to upgrade to a full Community Social Bank on the Clix platform. This gives you access to enhanced features, higher limits, and official recognition as a community banking institution.

Official status Enhanced features Bank partnership
